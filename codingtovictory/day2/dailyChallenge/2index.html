JP's Den (for real)

Freelance Consumer of Jazz Music

I am a native of Dallas, TX but have lived in the Seattle area since '95.
I have a background in anti-racist community organizing which began with the Black Prisoners' Caucus (BPC.org) at Monroe-WSRU and was deepened with the People's Institute for Survival and Beyond (PISAB.org) and the Village of Hope (Villageofhopeseattle.org), where I gained an anaylsis and practical experience applying that anaylsis in my personal and professional life.
My day job is doing racial equity work (institutional).
However, I remain connected to a base of anti-racist organizers and movements committed to racial justice.
I have two grown sons who are my hereos in many ways.
Good jazz and hip hop music feed my soul. And, I'm always in search of delicious Black coffee.

I became aware of this Victory Code Bootcamp from an email blast which came from being introduced to an Unloop co-founder a few years ago. I am nervous, grateful, and excited to be here. I'm hoping to gain the skills to support small community based organizations and social justice movements with their presence on the Web.
